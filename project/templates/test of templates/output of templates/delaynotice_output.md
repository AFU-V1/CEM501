Date of Delay Event
- March 12, 2026

Date Contractor Became Aware
- March 12, 2026

Location / Area Affected
- Foundation Section F-12, Basement Level B2

Description of Event
- Delivery of reinforcement steel for foundation section F-12 did not occur on the scheduled date of March 12, 2026. The supplier advised that the shipment was delayed in transit and could not reach site as planned.

Cause of Delay
- The steel supplier experienced transportation disruption due to severe weather conditions affecting regional logistics operations and road access to the project site.

Delay Classification
- Excusable; non-compensable; non-concurrent

Affected Activities
- Activity F12-R: Reinforcement installation for foundation section F-12
- Activity F12-C: Concrete pour for foundation section F-12
- Critical Path Impact: Yes - the delayed reinforcement delivery affects the critical path concrete pour sequence for Foundation Section F-12.

Affected Milestone / Completion Impact
- Foundation works milestone scheduled for March 20, 2026 may be delayed by 2 working days.

Estimated Duration of Delay
- 2 working days

Mitigation Measures
- The contractor is coordinating with the supplier for expedited delivery.
- Additional steel fixing labor will be assigned upon material arrival.
- The concrete pour sequence is under review to reduce downstream impact.

Cost Impact
- Potential additional labor and supervision costs associated with recovery measures are to be determined.

Reservation of Rights
- The Contractor hereby reserves the right to submit a formal claim for time extension and/or additional costs arising from this delay event, in accordance with General Conditions, Clause 8.4 - Notice of Delay and General Conditions, Clause 20.1 - Claims.

Requested Action
- Please acknowledge receipt of this Delay Notice and confirm whether any additional supporting documentation is required.
