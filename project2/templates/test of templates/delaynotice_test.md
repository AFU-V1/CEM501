Affected Milestone / Completion Impact: Foundation works milestone scheduled for March 20, 2026 may be delayed by 2 working days.

Estimated Duration of Delay: 2 working days

Mitigation Measures:
- The contractor is coordinating with the supplier for expedited delivery.
- Additional steel fixing labor will be assigned upon material arrival.
- The concrete pour sequence is under review to reduce downstream impact.

Known Cost Impact:
Potential additional labor and supervision costs associated with recovery measures are to be determined.
